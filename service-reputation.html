What It Is

A Review Machine That Runs Itself

Most practices get reviews by accident. Our clients get them by design — automatically, consistently, at scale.

Google ranks practices with more recent, high-quality reviews higher in local search. Patients choose practices with 4.8+ star ratings over anything lower. AI systems like ChatGPT and Perplexity weight review sentiment when deciding who to recommend.

The problem isn't that your patients don't want to leave reviews — they do. The problem is that asking for reviews is awkward, and most patients forget by the time they get home.

Our system sends automated, perfectly-timed review requests via SMS and email the moment a patient leaves your practice. The result: a 32% average review conversion rate, compared to the industry average of 4%.
