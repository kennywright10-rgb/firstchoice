What It Is

The New Word of Mouth Is AI

By 2026, more than 30% of local service searches start on AI platforms rather than Google. We make sure your practice wins every one of those searches.

AI language models like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity synthesize information from across the web to answer questions. When a patient asks "what's the best med spa near me," the AI pulls data from your website content, reviews, news mentions, directory listings, and social proof to decide who to recommend.

Most practices have fragmented, inconsistent, or thin content across these sources — which means AI either doesn't mention them, or mentions them without confidence. We fix that systematically.

Our AI Recommendation Architecture builds the exact structure these models look for: clear expertise signals, comprehensive service coverage, authoritative citations, and consistent entity data — so AI systems have no reason to recommend anyone but you.
