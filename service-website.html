What It Is

The Core 30 Website Framework

30 specific signals that make your website rank higher, convert better, and get recommended by AI — all built into a single, fast-loading site.

Most med spa websites fail on three fronts simultaneously: they don't rank (missing technical SEO), they don't convert (weak trust signals and CTAs), and they don't get recommended by AI (no structured data or content depth).

The Core 30 framework is our proprietary checklist of 30 website signals we've validated across 200+ med spa websites. Each signal independently improves ranking, trust, or conversion — and together, they compound into a site that works around the clock.

We either optimize your existing site or build a new one from scratch using the framework. Either way, the result is a website that earns its place in your marketing system — not just a digital brochure.
