What It Is

Your Practice, Visible to Every Patient Who's Looking

Google Maps isn't random. It's a ranking system — and like any ranking system, it has rules. We know every rule, every signal, and every lever that moves the needle in local med spa searches.

When a potential patient searches "Botox near me" or "best med spa in [your city]," Google decides which 3 practices to show in the Map Pack. That decision is based on hundreds of signals — your profile completeness, citation consistency, review velocity, proximity signals, and more.

Most practices have 60–70% of these signals working against them without knowing it. Inconsistent NAP data, an unclaimed GBP, stale photos, no service descriptions — any one of these suppresses your ranking.

We audit every signal, fix every gap, and implement an ongoing strategy that pushes you into the top 3 and keeps you there — even as competitors try to catch up.
